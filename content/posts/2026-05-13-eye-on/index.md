As I scroll through the internet, I save things I am interested in buying to a pinterest board called "shopping." Then, I regularly return to that board and review whether those things still actually call to me, or if I was driven by a momentary urge. Here are some things I've saved recently!

- **Cozy lighting improvements**: desired for all around the apartment.
    - To solve for the dark corner blocked by our L-shaped couch: [these neon tubes](https://www.echoneon.com/product/led-lamp/) might be interesting to hang vertically? Or maybe [something that calls less attention to itself](https://www.ikea.com/us/en/p/nymane-wall-up-downlight-white-00397859/)?
    - Just a [nice table lamp](https://kismas.com/products/orbital-lamp-01). Sadly I don't think we have a surface to hold this.
    - This [giraffe light](https://www.ikea.com/us/en/p/grejsimojs-led-decorative-light-floor-giraffe-yellow-battery-operated-00602039/) is very cute, yeah???

- **To add whimsy and art:** new [botanical print](https://www.josephkraft.com/shop/p/wildflowerlilac-9f6bz-3ylmx-fjfly-cdd8e-fxk2z-8lxab-f9p2f-9rrt5-mellp) from Chicago artist Joseph Kraft.

- **To upgrade my work-from-home set-up** in anticipation of the end of my parental leave: [a portable second monitor](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CH9WTW56). I currently have an old-style monitor that works fine - but something portable like this would let me have a couple of different work stations around the apartment.

- **To rest my weary head:** As a stomach/side sleeper extremely dissatisfied with my current pillow array, I'm intrigued about the [unique shape of this one](https://eliandelm.com/product/side-sleeper-pillow/).

- **To step up our dining experience:** new [Muji silverware](https://www.muji.us/products/idee-jasper-morrison-cutlery-8-piece-set-yb1sha4a). I always like spoons with bowls that are more rounded than oval. To be honest, though, this is a bit more than I'm willing to pay right now.
