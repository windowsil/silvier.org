Hi, I'm Silvie Senauke. I like to make weird little things, like this blog. Check out the [Craft Museum](/gallery) to see some of the other things I've made.

I live in Chicago with my husband and son. This site is part of my personal maternity leave agenda — a way to feel connected to creating and my own identity while caring for a tiny helpless being!

I'm taking inspiration from a couple of sites I enjoy most: [kottke.org](https://kottke.org) (short posts, a window into the strange interesting random parts of the world); [Cup of Jo](https://cupofjo.com) (style, highlights of objects she loves, parenting); [The Strategist](https://nymag.com/strategist) (things I might want to buy, product reviews).

Reach out to me at [hello@silvier.org](mailto:hello@silvier.org) with your comments or questions!
